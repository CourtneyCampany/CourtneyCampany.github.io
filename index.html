Hello! I am a PhD student at the Hawkesbury Institute for the Environment at the University of Western Sydney.

My research focuses on carbon and resouce allocation in trees across multiple scales. I have a diverse research background from ecosystem ecology to plant physiology and have worked on a variety of large scale climate change experiments in the United States and Australia.
